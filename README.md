## Introduction

Zeroflx is a modern, cross-platform video player powered by MPV and VLC playback engines, supporting virtually all video formats. It features seamless cross-device configuration synchronization with a clean, elegant user interface. Our mission is to deliver a smooth and consistent video playback experience.

If you encounter any issues or have feature requests, we welcome your feedback. Your support and suggestions are our greatest motivation to continue improving.
